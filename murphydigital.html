    A New Way to do Business

    Artificial intelligence tools such as ChatGPT have introduced an entirely new way to operate businesses. From automating outreach, to appointment booking and lead qualification, there are nearly infinite ways to apply AI in almost any business.

    This is where my agency steps in. We offer consultation with business owners on how to use AI themselves to specifically solve problems that their business faces, as well as offer our own complete and custom AI solutions to help automate major yet monotonous parts of business operations. Our objective in all that we do is to help educate business owners on how AI can be used to unlock the true potential of their organisation, and help them improve their effectiveness and longevity by keeping up to date with this cutting-edge technology.

    Some ways in which we have been able to improve business effectiveness for clients is through implementing chat bots on websites or in Facebook messenger in order to qualify leads and expand clientele, automated appointment booking, as well as online reputation management with an automated message to clients following appointments asking for a review.

    The one thing I am most proud of in terms of the differentiation of Murphy Digital from other AI Agencies in the space is that we offer a custom software package to our clients that is an all-in-one solution for managing business workflows and pipelines. This comes part and parcel with our services, and immensely simplifies the behind-the-scenes operations of businesses.
